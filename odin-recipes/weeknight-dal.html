Quick Weeknight Dal Recipe

Description

One dish has already been published here: Palak paneer with halloumi, another one was this recipe for Dal (Indian lentil stew). Well, “dal” is actually a Hindi word for all dried pulses (lentils, beans, peas). And even if “dal” is the spelling wikipedia and other sources tend to use most, there are plenty of variations like; daal, dahl and dhal.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • olive oil
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • 500 g crushed tomatoes
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 2 cubes vegetable bouillon
  • 300 ml red lentils
  • 100 ml water
  • 70 g spinach
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • chipotle chili
  • black pepper
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  1. Chop onion and garlic and fry in a big pan until translucent
  2. Add the cinnamon sticks and other spices and let them fry for 1 min while stirring
  3. Mix in tomatoes, coconut milk and bouillon. Bring to a boil
  4. Add the lentils and let it simmer for 10 min with lid on. Lentils should be a little al dente
  5. Add spinach and let it wilt. Add the lemon then taste and adjust to your liking
  6. Serve hot with some bread
